## 🚀 HOW TO USE NEW FEATURES:

### View Stories:
1. Go to `/feed`
2. Click on any story circle at top
3. Watch the story
4. Auto-advances or click "Next"

### React to Posts:
1. Hover over "Like" button
2. Popup appears with reactions
3. Click your reaction
4. Get XP!

### Create a Poll:
1. Click "📊 Poll" button when creating post
2. Enter question
3. Add options (2 minimum)
4. Post it!

### Toggle Theme:
1. Click ☀️/🌙 button (bottom right)
2. Instant theme switch
3. Preference saved

### Check Achievements:
1. Click "🏆 Achievements" in sidebar
2. See unlocked achievements
3. Check progress on locked ones
4. View leaderboard

### Play Music:
1. Click 🎵 in top nav
2. Music player appears at bottom
3. Use controls to play/pause/skip
